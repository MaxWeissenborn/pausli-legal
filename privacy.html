Privacy policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

The short version

Pausli is deliberately built so that your data stays with you. Everything you enter in the app — discomfort entries, completed exercises, your streak, your settings — is stored exclusively on your device. There are no user accounts, no servers of ours, no analytics or tracking services, and no ads. We never have access to your content.

What the app stores locally

  • Discomfort entries (body region, intensity, time): these are health data within the meaning of Art. 9 GDPR. You enter them voluntarily; they are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to us or to third parties.
  • Completed exercises (exercise, time, duration) — for your streak, history, and suggestions.
  • Your settings (work days, work hours, reminder interval, language, appearance).
  • The time of first installation — it drives the 7-day trial and is kept in your device's secure storage (iOS: Keychain, Android: encrypted preferences). On iOS this entry can survive a reinstall.

The legal basis for this local processing is providing the app features you request (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR); health-related entries are only recorded when you actively enter them (Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR).

Notifications

For break reminders the app asks for permission to send you notifications. Reminders are scheduled entirely on your device; no push service and no server is involved. You can revoke the permission at any time in your system settings.

Purchases and subscriptions

Subscriptions are handled exclusively by the Apple App Store or Google Play. Apple and Google process your payment data as independent controllers under their own privacy policies. We receive no payment or account data — the app only stores locally whether an active subscription exists. Completing a purchase briefly requires an internet connection; apart from opening links, this is the app's only network communication.

No sharing, no tracking

Your data is not transmitted to third parties, not sold, and not processed for advertising or analytics. The app contains no tracking, analytics, or crash-reporting services and sets no cookies. When you open a source link in the app you leave the app, and the privacy policy of that website applies.

Export and deletion

At any time you can export all entries as a JSON file or delete them permanently under Settings → "My data" — including after the trial has ended. Uninstalling the app removes your data as well (exception on iOS: the installation timestamp in the Keychain mentioned above may persist; it contains no health or usage data).

Your rights

You have the rights under Art. 15–21 GDPR (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, objection). Because your data lives exclusively on your device and we have no access to it, you can exercise access, portability, and erasure directly in the app (export and delete functions). For questions, contact us at the email address above. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR).

Changes

If the app or the legal situation changes, we will update this policy. The current version is always available in the app under Settings → Privacy policy and on this page.
